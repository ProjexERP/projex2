Key Benefits of Manage Account

Users can update their details, set preferences, and manage access — all from one place. This reduces dependency on admin teams for basic account updates and streamlines daily operations.

With built-in tools like password management, Two-Factor Authentication (2FA), and activity tracking, ProjexERP ensures that every user has a secure environment tailored to their role. It protects sensitive business data and reduces the risk of unauthorized access.

Role-Based Access and Accountability

The ability to assign roles and permissions ensures that users only access data relevant to their job functions. This minimizes errors, strengthens data integrity, and enhances accountability within the system.

Quick access to help resources and support channels means users spend less time solving issues and more time doing their work — improving overall system usability.
