Key Features of Sale Ordering

ProjexERP streamlines your sales process with a powerful and intuitive Sale Ordering module. Designed to enhance accuracy, speed, and control, our system helps businesses manage their customer orders efficiently from initiation to fulfillment.

Quick Order Entry

ProjexERP makes sales order creation fast and hassle-free. Users can generate orders within seconds using a streamlined interface that intelligently auto-fills customer details, pricing, and tax rules. This reduces manual input, speeds up the process, and minimizes errors—making every transaction smoother and more efficient.

Real-Time Inventory Integration

Stay updated with live inventory levels while placing orders. The system checks stock availability in real time, ensuring accurate fulfillment planning. As soon as an order is confirmed, inventory is reserved automatically, helping prevent overselling and stockouts.

Multi-Level Fulfillment

Manage complex deliveries with ease. ProjexERP allows you to split sales orders by warehouse or location, making multi-site order processing seamless. The system also auto-generates all necessary documents—like picking lists, shipping labels, and invoices—to accelerate fulfillment.

Dynamic Pricing & Discounts

Offer flexible, customer-centric pricing strategies. The Sales Ordering module supports dynamic pricing based on client type, purchase volume, or ongoing promotions. Tax calculations are automated and location-aware, ensuring compliance and accurate billing every time.

Order History & Repeat Orders

Access a complete record of previous orders for any customer. This feature simplifies reordering by allowing users to duplicate frequent or contract-based orders with just one click, improving service speed and customer satisfaction

Approval Workflows

Ensure order control with customizable approval processes. Large or high-risk sales can be routed through specific workflows, where limits and conditions trigger alerts and require authorization before proceeding. This adds an essential layer of oversight and accountability.
